Univision - March 2023

Communictions Engineer

As the communications engineer for Univision I came up with the plan for the entirety of the production and allowed for 30+ people to communicate perfectly during the production. This involved configuring an RTS Odin and a mixture of Omneo and non Omneo devices and using dante to patch in device from another location half a mile away.

Live From Surrey - December 2022

Sound Guarantee and Broadcast Mix

On live from Surrey I worked as Sound Guarantee setting up the audio network between mulitple locations, patching the dante network to the Ravenna network for the Ravenna desk we were using for broadcast.

Vice-Chancellor’s Awards - November 2022

Broadcast Mix

Worked with the team doing live sound in the room to co-ordinate dante channels to be sent over fibre to the TV Studio. Then ensured that the dante was converted to multicast flows for the Ravenna compatible mixing desk to be able to use.

Rodulfus Christmas Virtual Choir - December 2020

Sound Editor

Worked in a small team of audio editors on editting 40-60 singers who had recorded audio at home. My job was to ensure that they were all in time, with their silibances and plosives all being lined up, and that they all came off of notes together. The specific piece I editted are God Rest Ye Merry Gentlemen (06:46), Jingle Bells (27:36) and, Hark the Herald (33:50).
